ORBIT — Privacy & Data Practices

Effective date: May 5, 2026 · Version 1.0

This document describes how ORBIT handles information on your device and when optional services are enabled. It is a plain-language summary of our practices for the ORBIT mobile application.

1. Local-first design

Much of your schedule, discipline data, profile preferences, and optional name or photo are stored locally on your iPhone using app storage (including encrypted device backup subject to your device settings).

2. Data you provide

Examples include:

  • Schedule & habits: wake/sleep, blocks, habits, completions, and related scoring inputs.
  • Optional profile: first name, last name, and optional profile photo (processed and stored on-device).
  • Physical baseline: metrics used for training calibration (e.g. height/weight targets)—not a substitute for medical records.
  • Financial/study baselines: onboarding inputs used to shape scheduling.

3. Calendar & reminders

If you choose Apple Calendar or Google Calendar, ORBIT may create or update calendar events to mirror your schedule and (where enabled) deliver reminders. Those providers process data under their privacy policies.

4. Payments

Subscription status is validated through Apple (StoreKit). ORBIT does not receive your full payment card number.

5. Analytics & logging

Diagnostic logging may be written to the system console on your device for troubleshooting. ORBIT is not used for cross-app advertising in the current product direction.

6. Future social features

Features such as friend chat may require additional categories of data (e.g. message content, identifiers). ORBIT will describe those practices before enabling such features.

7. Retention & deletion

Deleting ORBIT data via in-app Delete Account / reset removes locally persisted ORBIT payload keys on the device to the extent implemented; mirrored calendar events may need separate cleanup in Calendar apps.

8. Children’s privacy

ORBIT is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).

9. Contact & updates

Contact the developer using the support URL listed on the ORBIT App Store listing. This document may be updated; the effective date will change when it does.
